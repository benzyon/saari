Rules

Main Content

Basic

This is supposed to be a light hearted roleplay. Characters can talk about their dark past and all that but please leave the dark stuff back in there world. Character come to this roleplay room through sleeping, getting knocked unconscious or other means of passing out.

Characters who are not in the database will be silenced until they are in the data base. You can still lurk and not enter the room with the character not in the data base however do not join until they are registered. Its more of a security measure and to play responsibily on the player.

Character Descriptions

Please put your characters age, Gender, and other noteable descriptions on the side status in the chat. There is a place to copy and paste the status on your characters page and description.

Staying in world/Timing out

Your characters are not allowed to stay in world. If you are away from your keyboard for over two hours we will assume your character disappeared back to their world. Characters are also not allowed to live in the rp world. If you are going to be gone for less than two hours but need to go do something, just have your character go take a name or something.

Characters can NOT go to other characters worlds

There is no possibly way for a character to go to another characters world. Even if your character is a god, all powerful, or even a universe jumper, they can not go to the other characters world to change the others' story.

Places you can have your character "appear"

1. Under a tree near the lake

2. On the road near the house

3. Any where around the house at least 20 feet away

4.  In the forest within 100 feet of the house

5. If they are a flying character they can suddenly be gliding near the house

6. if they are a water character they can be in the lake

 

How your character gets to the world

Your character fell asleep in their world and sudenly ended up in this one. Its like a dream, they don't know how they got there, they just are.

When your character returns to their world

When your characters go back to their world, they don't remember anything from the rp until they return. Once they return, they remember everything that has happened there [well as much as your remember]

Your Characters can not take items back with them that they didnt take with them

If someone takes something, it is left behind when they disappear. If someone eats something it will reappear where it was taken from, untouched. If they give someone something, it will fade out when they do.

This is not meant to be a stressful

Keep the drama to a minimum. If it gets too crazy tell the person causing it to please stop or if you are too shy to do that you can change characters or try and contact someone else to talk with them. it is meant to be fun and a place to develop characters.

Along with that DONT BE A JERK

This is a romance free roleplay

This is a place for characters to meet and make friends and get developted. Its not for characters to find their soulmate or significant other. If you and the other author decide that your characters are perfect for each other, make a seperate NON CANNON room. Relationships start unnecessary drama in the room.

No sex in any shape or form.

This is not an R/NR roleplay room. If your character cant keep it in their pants then dont bring them in. If you and the other author want your characters to get it on, like the relationship thing, NON CANNON room.

No killing characters with out permission

Dont kill each others characters unless the author gives you permission. If your character does get 'killed' they dont actually die, they wake up in their world as if you died in a dream though they dont remember. If you have a murderous character, unless they can control themselves, dont bring them in please.

The place cleans it self

They place has magic cleaning roombas that activate automatically when ever something gets dirty, is spilt, breaks, ect. All the floors are hard wood and the roombas can climb walls if something was splattered against them.

Time of day

The time of day and season in the given area never change. The time of day is always going to be about 2-3 in the afternoon [14-15 military time]. However in the black forest it is always dark though not dark enough that you cant see.

The temperature never changes

There are areas with different temperatures. The weather is always partly cloudy unless a character changes it along with the temperature. The different temperatures per area are shown below

Swearing is allowed

Just try not to go overboard with it though I'm not going to be strict about it. I will be if its making other authors uncomfortable.
